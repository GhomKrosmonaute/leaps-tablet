Training Session

Your goal is to explore the space of "shifting shapes"
and to discover shapes that you find interesting and beautiful.

The shapes are composed of connected squares.

At each step you can move one square from the current shape to create a new shape.

Drag the left-most or right-most square to a different place.

Only squares that are at the edge of the shape are movable


You can do as many moves as you like.

When you find a shape that you like you can collect it
to a 'Gallery Space' by clicking on the ‘Collect Shape’ button.

Click there now

At the last stage of the game you will see all the shapes
you have stored in the 'Gallery Space' and will be asked
to choose among them.

The game is 12 minutes long.
Please play alone, in a quiet surroundings with no interruptions.

Thanks and enjoy the game!
